# Draft Appearance Traits

Appearance traits are fictional prospect metadata for future AI portrait prompts.
They include eye color, ethnicity-weighted hair color and hairstyle, facial hair
style, and one or occasionally two ethnicity descriptors.

The generator keeps most players to one ethnicity descriptor. A small share get
a secondary descriptor, while players in the "Two or More Races" bucket always
receive two photo-oriented descriptors.

Facial hair rates are tuned for college-aged prospects: clean shaven, stubble,
and short beard are common; mustache-only, chinstrap, and patchy beard are rare
outliers. Older prospects get a small age-based nudge toward stubble and fuller
beards, while 20-21 year olds skew slightly cleaner.

Hairstyles are weighted by the generated ethnicity bucket for portrait flavor,
with a small outlier lane for less common looks.

Hair color is separate from hairstyle. Normal colors include black, dark brown,
brown, light brown, blond, auburn, and rare red hair.
